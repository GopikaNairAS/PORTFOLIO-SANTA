CHRISTMAS

Christmas,the birthday of Jesus Christ, is a season filled with warmth, lights, and the quiet joy of being with people we love. Homes smell of sweets and pine, and even ordinary days feel a little more magical. It reminds us to pause for a moment, share kindness, and be grateful for the small moments that matter most.


Through my service,I am a symbol of giving and joy during Christmas, bringing smiles to children around the world. On Christmas Eve, I deliver gifts to spread happiness and keep the magic alive. Through my journey, I remind everyone to be kind, generous, and to believe in the spirit of Christmas.
